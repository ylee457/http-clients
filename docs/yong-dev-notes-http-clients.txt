Excel date auto increment 

Here's how to do it:

    Put the starting date and the first increment in two rows (ex: Row 1: 5/1/08 Row 2: 5/2/08).
    Select the two rows.
    Move your mouse to the bottom right corner of the second row. The cursor should turn into a plus sign.
    Move the plus sign down until the dates have incremented to where you want.
